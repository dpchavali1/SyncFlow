# SyncFlow Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

SyncFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SyncFlow applications for Android, macOS, and web platforms.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you pair devices, we may collect device identifiers and pairing information
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers and contact names for messaging functionality
- **Message Content**: SMS and MMS content for synchronization between devices

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, feature usage, and performance metrics
- **Network Information**: IP addresses and connection information for Firebase services

### 1.3 Third-Party Services
We use Firebase (Google) for real-time data synchronization and authentication. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- To synchronize messages, calls, and notifications between your devices
- To provide real-time communication features
- To maintain device pairing and authentication

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- To analyze app performance and user experience
- To debug and fix technical issues
- To develop new features and improvements

### 2.3 Legal Compliance
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- To protect our rights and prevent fraud

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We may share information with Firebase and other service providers necessary for app functionality
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Encryption
- All data transmitted between devices is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- Message content is encrypted end-to-end when possible
- Firebase communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

### 4.2 Data Storage
- Message content is temporarily stored in Firebase for synchronization
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for service functionality
- You can request deletion of your data at any time

### 4.3 Device Security
- Data is processed locally on your devices when possible
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Data**: Retained while you use the service and maintain device pairing
- **Inactive Data**: Automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity
- **Backup Data**: May be retained for up to 90 days for recovery purposes

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Data Access and Control
- You can view and manage your paired devices within the app
- You can unpair devices to stop data synchronization
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data

### 6.2 Opt-Out Options
- Disable notification mirroring in app settings
- Disable clipboard synchronization in app settings
- Limit permissions granted to the Android app

### 6.3 Data Deletion
To request deletion of your data, contact us at syncflow.contact@gmail.com with your user ID.

## 7. Children's Privacy

SyncFlow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- Email: syncflow.contact@gmail.com
- Website: https://syncflow.app/privacy
